How it all began

It all started from a lucid dream Kase Fenley had when he was 17. Where he realized he was wearing something he had designed in the future. He quickly woke up and drafted the concept down.

From this moment on he began studying the brain with the idea: how do humans create something from thought into matter? And, how could we use fashion to protect what is most vital. Life
